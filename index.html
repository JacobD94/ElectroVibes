My love for Electronic Music

I don't know what is actually was up with me. Back in the mid 2000s, when I was just a boy right before puberty hit me. I considered it not real music even though it follows the standard 4/4 time signature. Being in band but an elitist and not a true music conessuer, I pushed it to the side. Even many metal and rock bands were considered mussic to me, even though I barely listened to it. It took me until 2012 when I was in high school and getting to know the opposite sex a big better I gravitated towards electronic music. It was both intellectually stimulating and it kept me calm whearas with metal at a younger age I was so numbed by it it nearly had no effect it seemed.
